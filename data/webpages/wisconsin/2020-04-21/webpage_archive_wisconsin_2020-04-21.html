COVID-19 (Coronavirus): Persons in our Care Testing Dashboard

Content_Area1

​

  • ​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​COVID-19 Home
​​

​​​

The Wisconsin Department of Corrections remains committed to maintaining transparency throughout the duration of the public health emergency to keep the public informed about the health and well-being of those who live and work in our facilities. The data below represents the latest testing and case information amongst Persons in our Care and will be updated at 4:00 p.m. each weekday.

This interactive dashboard contains data regarding COVID-19 testing across the adult correctional facilities. Click on the different tabs at the top of the dashboard to view additional detailed information including testing trends, testing maps and a breakdown of test results by location.

NDCS Coronavirus Health Update

The Nebraska Department of Correctional Services (NDCS) is working to help minimize the transmission of coronavirus also known as COVID-19. Team members and people incarcerated in its facilities have been advised that sanitary precautions are currently the best defense against contracting the illness. For more information on what NDCS is doing, click the links below.

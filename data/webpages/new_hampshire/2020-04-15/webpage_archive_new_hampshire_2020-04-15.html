COVID-19 (Corona Virus)

We have been engaged and continue to be engaged in preventative measures regarding COVID-19. We know this is an uncertain and stressful time. Please be assured our focus is on the health and wellness of residents and our staff. If you have questions, please e-mail them to feedback@doc.nh.gov so that we can assist you rather than direct phones calls if possible. This will allow our staff to stay focused on the daily operations and let our public information staff respond to your inquiries.

As updates are available, we will add them here as well as our social media outets.
